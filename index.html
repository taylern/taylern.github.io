Personal Website of Tayler Nichol

Welcome!

I am a student at Fleming College and an aspiring geospatial scientist. This website is intended to host my personal projects, a short description of my qualifications, and eventually a blog with science writing on GIS, climate change, and the natural environment.

I am a recent graduate of the Environmental Geoscience and Bioarchaeological Anthropology programs at the University of Western Ontario, and I have previously worked as an environmental planner and a geological consultant. My interest in mapping stems from my interest in combining art and science to analyze and visualize information.
